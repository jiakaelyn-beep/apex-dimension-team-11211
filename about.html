About Our Team

Apex Dimension Team 11211 is a robotics team dedicated to inspiring students through engineering, programming, and teamwork. Members collaborate to design, build, and program robots while gaining hands-on experience in STEM fields.


Our team participates in robotics competitions where we test our robot designs and strategies against teams from across the region. Through this process, students learn problem solving, innovation, and leadership.
